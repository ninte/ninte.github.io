About

Hi! I'm Héctor, a researcher passionate about applied artificial intelligence, born in Alcalá de Henares. My interest in technology started at a young age, largely influenced by my high school technology teacher who passed on his enthusiasm and knowledge to me. This led me to embark on a career in technology, a field that fascinates me and where I have found my true vocation.

Beyond my work, I enjoy immersing myself in activities that allow me to disconnect, such as rock climbing and hiking in nature. I am also a lover of music and video games, which give me the opportunity to explore new worlds.

In my life and work, I deeply value companionship, collaboration, transparency and effort. These principles not only guide my research, but also define how I relate to others.

In addition, I love experimenting with technology in my day-to-day life. In fact, I have domotized a large part of my home, a personal project that has allowed me to integrate my love for innovation with my daily life.

Resume

After completing my PhD in Information and Communication Technologies in the field of autonomous driving, vehicle detection, keypoint extraction and fine-grained vehicle classification in 2021, I joined NielsenIQ as a Machine Learning Research Scientist.

During my 3 years at NielsenIQ, I have worked in the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP) applied to Key Information Extraction (KIE) from receipts and invoices, Product Matching, Categorization, and various other business processes. My work involved researching and developing innovative solutions to automate and enrich NIQ's processes and data.
